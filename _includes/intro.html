Hi, we're CoderDojo York a fortnightly programming club for young people aged 7-17 in York. Learn how to code, develop apps and games, and most importantly have fun doing it!

We are sponsored by the software development agency, Recollate.

and by Jammed - Music studio and rehearsal room booking software.
